About CEGP

Championing press freedom and nurturing the next generation of Filipino journalists since 1952.

Who We Are

The College Editors Guild of the Philippines (CEGP) is a national organization of student publications and campus journalists. Founded in 1952, it is one of the oldest and most respected student press organizations in the country, advocating for academic freedom, press freedom, and the rights of student journalists.

Our Core Values
🗞️
Press Freedom
We uphold the constitutional right of all journalists to report freely and without fear.
🎓
Academic Excellence
We champion high standards of journalism in all campus publications nationwide.
✊
Student Rights
We defend student editors against censorship, harassment, and intimidation.
🤝
Solidarity
One voice for the Filipino student press across all schools and regions.
